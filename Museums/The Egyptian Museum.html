Activities

The Director-General of UNESCO at the Egyptian Museum

In April 2021, Ms. Audrey Azoulay, the Director-General of UNESCO, paid a field visit to Old Cairo, one of the World Heritage Sites, including the Egyptian Museum in Cairo, as part of a two-day mission to Egypt.

The Permanent Exhibition "The Hidden Treasures: The Coffins

In August 2021, the permanent exhibition "The Hidden Treasures: The Coffins" was opened in celebration of the 118th anniversary of the Egyptian Museum's opening on November 17, 2020. This exhibition was organized after the transfer of the mummies to permanent display at the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization. The mummy hall and its corridor were completely renovated to display the wooden coffins found in the Deir el-Bahari cache, the Cache of the Priests of Amun, and the Cache of the Priests of Montu. The exhibition also includes a collection of funerary artifacts, ritual offerings, funerary and religious papyri, and other tomb relics.
